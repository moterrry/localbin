Privacy Policy

At Localbin, we believe that your code is your business. Our service is designed with a "Local First" and "Privacy First" philosophy.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell a single piece of your personal data. There are no tracking cookies, no analytics scripts, and no user accounts.

Local Storage

All pastes you create are stored directly on the server host's filesystem. We do not use third-party databases or cloud storage providers. Your data remains exactly where you put it.

Encryption & Security

  • Password Protection: If you choose to password protect a paste, we hash it using bcrypt before it ever touches our storage. We never store plain-text passwords.
  • Auto-Expiration: You have full control over how long your data exists. Once a paste expires, it is permanently deleted from the filesystem.

Transparency

Localbin is a transparent tool. What you see is what you get. We don't hide any background processes. Your privacy isn't just a policy—it's how the tool is built.
